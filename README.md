## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 1. [Pixel Compass](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.github.fertwbr.pixelcompass) 🧭
- **Status:** Launched (43,000+ Installs) 
- **Description:** A premium navigation and environmental utility for **Android and Wear OS**. It features a highly interactive UI, advanced sensor fusion (True North, GPS Elevation), configurable home screen widgets, and smart meteorological insights.
- **Tech Stack:** **Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Wear OS, Jetpack Glance (Widgets), Retrofit & Moshi, SensorManager, Hilt, Play Billing.**

### 2. [Pixel Pulse](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.github.fertwbr.pixelpulse) 🎛️
- **Status:** Launched
- **Description:** A sophisticated sound level meter and WHO-compliant hearing health coach for **Android and Wear OS**. It calculates real-time "Noise Dose," tracks cumulative exposure across devices with "Zero-Gap" synchronization, and features military-grade encrypted backups (`.ppbk`).
- **Tech Stack:** **Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Wear OS, AudioRecord & FFT (JTransforms), Room Database (Pre-aggregations), WorkManager/Foreground Services.**

### 3. [fertwbr.com (App Ecosystem Portal)](https://fertwbr.com/) 🌐
